I am a firm believer in staying physically active on a daily basis in order to maintain physical strength, especially when it is difficult to give up or create. Playing games or exercising help control body weight. Exercise can aid in the prevention or maintenance of weight loss, as well as the treatment of medical conditions and seasonal diseases. It improves sleep, increases energy, and makes life more enjoyable and social. My training sessions are typically held at UW-Madison, WI.

Regular Workouts

 

Make it a habit to exercise every day for better overall health and fitness. Achieving optimal health and the ideal physique is all about developing long-term habits.

Basketball Game

I started playing basketball in elementary school and have won numerous awards at the school, district, regional, and national levels. It allows me to burn calories (an hour of basketball can burn 630-750 calories), improve balance and coordination, focus and self-discipline, and build muscle. Finally, it gives me graceful memories.
